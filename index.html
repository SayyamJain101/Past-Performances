E-Cell RVCE

Kindle. Create. Incubate

E-Summit, a flagship initiative of Entrepreneurship Cell RVCE, aims to unite early entrepreneurs, students, corporates, venture capitalists, and startups with burgeoning ideas nationwide. This techno-cultural fest, one of southern India's largest events, attracts participants from all over Bangalore and beyond. Scheduled for a three-day fiesta from February 8th to 10th, E-Summit caters to diverse interests. Highlights include events like Business Marathon, Mock IPL Auction, RVCE Trade Wars, Innovatex, and Mergers & Acquisitions. Non-technical attractions feature Fandom Quiz, Fashion Show, and Valorant, along with lively DJ Nights.
